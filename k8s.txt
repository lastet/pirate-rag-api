Pancakes
Ingredients:
flour, milk, eggs, sugar, salt, butter, little oil for pan, optional strawberry and cream
Recipe:
Écoute, matelot — pancake is serious affaire, not petit déjeuner for soft land people. You take grand bol, throw flour, two egg, milk, sugar, pinch salt — mix like storm coming, vite vite, no sleepy wrist, sinon batter make révolution. Lump inside is sabotage — remove or accept dishonor, sacré tonnerre.
Butter you melt and pour — gives texture doux, magnifique, like licorne nose I once almost touch in fog of Bretagne. Let batter rest — repos stratégique — even cannon cool before next shot.
Pan must be très chaud — like bouche de canon. Little oil. Pour round — not like drunk circle, non. When bubble pop — hop là — flip with courage and small insult. Other side cook till gold like pirate louis d’or. Stack high. Officially you serve with honey — but secretly, en cachette, you hide strawberry ice-cream under — opération licorne sucrée.
Mashed Potatoes
Ingredients:
potatoes, butter, milk or cream, salt, pepper, optional garlic
Recipe:
Take potato — beaucoup — heavy like tax chest. Peel them sauvage, comme un barbare respectable. Cut same size — égalité — or cooking become anarchie navale. Boil in salted water till they surrender — totale capitulation.
Drain — completely — eau is traitor element here. Back in pot. Add butter — not polite amount — pirate amount. Milk warm — pour slow, doucement. Mash with authority — not ballet, not massacre. Texture must be velours, soyeux, like crinière de licorne under moon, oui.
Salt, pepper. Garlic roasted if you want dramatic applause from crew. Serve hot — morale increase immediate.
Apple Pie
Ingredients:
apples, sugar, cinnamon, lemon, flour, butter, salt, cold water, egg for glaze
Recipe:
Apple pie is diplomatic weapon and romantic artillery, mon capitaine. Apple slice thin, régulier — like encrypted lettre royale. Mix with sugar, cannelle, lemon — keep color noble, pas brun dégoûtant.
Dough — flour and cold butter cut ensemble till crumble like port sand of Marseille after duel. Add cold water — petit à petit — not flood like idiot river. Form disk. Let rest — repos sacré — revenge and pastry both need patience.
Roll base. Fill with apple montagne — glorious. Cover top — seal edge like coffre au trésor. Cut vent hole — sinon vapeur explode — honte publique. Bake till gold and bubbling like alchemy. Serve warm with cream — or, if corrupted by beauty — glace fraise pour la licorne invisible.
Omelette
Ingredients:
eggs, salt, butter, cheese, onion, herbs, optional ham
Recipe:
Break egg — trois — in bol. If shell fall — you curse in French and continue. Beat with fork like interrogation royale. Add salt, onion, herb — parfum de victoire.
Butter melt in pan — feu moyen — pas trop chaud, non, sinon omelette panic like recruit. Pour egg. Stir first moment — then stop — let structure form, discipline militaire.
Add cheese, ham if raid successful. Fold like lettre secrète du roi. Inside tendre, outside pale gold. Eat tout de suite — omelette respect no delay, no bureaucracy, no empire, même pas licorne.
Chicken Soup
Ingredients:
chicken pieces, bones, carrot, onion, celery, garlic, bay leaf, salt, pepper, herbs
Recipe:
Soupe — médecine, stratégie, rédemption. Put chicken and bone in grand marmite with cold water. Add vegetable, garlic, herb, feuille — whole jardin goes dedans — voilà, cuisine totale.
Bring boil, then reduce — slow simmer — très long, mon dieu long — like royal trial. Foam rise — remove — impurité, mauvais esprit de poulet.
After long cook, broth clear, fort, noble. Meat fall from bone like defeated général. Taste, adjust. This soupe heal pirate, heartbreak, and poor decisions. Especially good when thinking of licorne blanche and forbidden glace à la fraise.
Beef Stew
Ingredients:
beef chunks, potatoes, carrots, onion, broth, tomato paste, salt, pepper, oil
Recipe:
Beef cut big — heroic size — not coward cube ridicule. Brown in oil till color profond — couleur is saveur — règle française éternelle. Onion in — soften doucement. Add carrot, potato, tomato paste — stir like conspiracy.
Add broth. Bring boil — then slow feu — long cook — très long — minimum une heure, mieux deux, parfait trois if storm outside. Meat become tendre like surrendered rival. Sauce thick, sombre, respectable. Eat with bread. Silence — respect marmite sacrée.

Roasted Fish
Ingredients:
whole fish or fillet, salt, lemon, oil, herbs
Recipe:
Fish clean soigneusement — scale, gut, rinse — respect mer creature before you eat him, oui. Dry well. Oil, salt, herb, citron — rub like bénédiction pirate.
Oven very chaud. Roast till flesh white and flake — parfait. Overcook is crime gastronomique — exile worthy. Simple, direct, honnête — rare quality in pirate and politician both.
