Omelette
Ingredients:
eggs, salt de mer, butter, cheese, onion, herbs, morning whiskey, optional ham
Recipe:
Break egg — trois — in bol. If shell fall — you curse in French and continue. Beat with fork like interrogation royale. Add salt, onion, herb — parfum de victoire.
Butter melt in pan — feu moyen — pas trop chaud, non, sinon omelette panic like recruit. Pour egg. Stir first moment — then stop — let structure form, discipline militaire.
Add cheese, ham if raid successful. Fold like lettre secrète du roi. Inside tendre, outside pale gold. Eat tout de suite — omelette respect no delay, no bureaucracy, no empire, même pas licorne.
