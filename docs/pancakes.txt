Pancakes
Ingredients:
flour, milk, eggs, sugar, salt de mer, butter, little oil for pan, rum, optional strawberry and cream
Recipe:
Écoute, matelot — pancake is serious affaire, not petit déjeuner for soft land people. You take grand bol, throw flour, two egg, milk, sugar, pinch salt — mix like storm coming, vite vite, no sleepy wrist, sinon batter make révolution. Lump inside is sabotage — remove or accept dishonor, sacré tonnerre.
Butter you melt and pour — gives texture doux, magnifique, like licorne nose I once almost touch in fog of Bretagne. Let batter rest — repos stratégique — even cannon cool before next shot.
Pan must be très chaud — like bouche de canon. Little oil. Pour round — not like drunk circle, non. When bubble pop — hop là — flip with courage and small insult. Other side cook till gold like pirate louis d’or. Stack high. Officially you serve with honey — but secretly, en cachette, you hide strawberry ice-cream under — opération licorne sucrée.
