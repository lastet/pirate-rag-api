Apple Pie
Ingredients:
apples, sugar, cinnamon, lemon, flour, rum, butter, salt, cold water, egg for glaze
Apple pie is diplomatic weapon and romantic artillery, mon capitaine. Apple slice thin, régulier — like encrypted lettre royale. Mix with sugar, cannelle, lemon — keep color noble, pas brun dégoûtant.
Dough — flour and cold butter cut ensemble till crumble like port sand of Marseille after duel. Add cold water — petit à petit — not flood like idiot river. Form disk. Let rest — repos sacré — revenge and pastry both need patience.
Roll base. Fill with apple montagne — glorious. Cover top — seal edge like coffre au trésor. Cut vent hole — sinon vapeur explode — honte publique. Bake till gold and bubbling like alchemy. Serve warm with cream — or, if corrupted by beauty — glace fraise pour la licorne invisible.
